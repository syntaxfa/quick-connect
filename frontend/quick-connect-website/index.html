The Modern Platform for Customer Engagement.

Quick Connect is a suite of open-source, self-hostable services designed to create meaningful, real-time interactions with your users through chat, notifications, and more.

An Entire Communication Suite

Everything you need to build a modern communication experience.

Live Chat

Engage with your customers instantly using our low-latency chat widget, powered by WebSockets for a seamless, real-time experience.

Notification Engine

Keep users informed with our powerful, multi-channel notification service. Deliver messages via In-App, Push, Email, and SMS.

Story Platform

Engage users with ephemeral, full-screen content. Share updates, promotions, or news in a familiar and immersive format.
